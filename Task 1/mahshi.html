Have You Tried Mahshi Before

This my Middle Eastern “mahashi” is my favourite way to eat squash and eggplant–stuffed with spiced, herby meat and rice, then simmered in tomato sauce.

If you have roots in the Middle East or North Africa this is probably among your favourite comfort foods. If not, it will be once you try it.

In the Middle East people stuff everything. I mean everything. Any vegetable you can think, someone’s found a way to stuff it.
